BMI Information

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measurement of a person's weight in relation to their height. It provides a simple numeric measure of thinness or fatness. BMI is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters.

BMI Categories

BMI Category Health Risk
Below 18.5 Underweight Possible nutritional deficiency
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy Low risk
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight Moderate risk
30.0 and above Obese High risk
